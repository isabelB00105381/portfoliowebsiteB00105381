About Isabel:

Isabel is studying Creative Digital Media at ITB, she is over halfway through her first year and she is working diligently to complete the rest of the semester. She is interested in all of her modules but she sometimes finds the demands for various assignments tricky to manage.Isabel is almost 19 years old and her birthday is April the 26th.Isabel has a taste for adventure and travelling and she wants to enter careers that would allow her to travel around the world.Isabel is excited to learn new skills and expand on them and see where it takes her once she graduates from ITB.
Isabel is very creative and is keen to experiment with new ideas and oppertunties that she will meet while studying at ITB.She always tries to get assignments submitted on time and to of good quality and she prides herself on her time mangament skills.Isabel is also very committed to her bigger assignment submissions in each module.
Isabel remembers when she had to stay back at college all day until nine at night so that she could get one of her final assignments submitted on time because she had no access to certain programmes at home.She is quite enthusiastic when it comes to her work, she puts passion and effort into all her various projects and assigments.
